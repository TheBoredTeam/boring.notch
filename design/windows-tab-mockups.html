Where to push back

  • Direction — which one (or two-mixed) feels like Gojo? Hot take welcome.
  • Identity — is "Cursor · main.swift" too much, or not enough?
  • Empty state — replace identity with a dashed placeholder, or shrink to a hint?
  • Multi-display — show display pill always, only on multi-mon, or never inline?
  • Shortcuts — keep visible (current) or reveal on ⌘-hold?
  • Maximize — center tile (03), trailing chip (05), or its own hot corner?
